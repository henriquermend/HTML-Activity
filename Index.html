About the team

Imperial Esports, (formerly stylized as Imperial e-Sports) is a Brazilian professional e-sports organization.

It is best known for its Counter-Strike: Global Offensive team, which is made up of a cast dubbed by fans of "The Last Dance",initially featuring veteran players from the Brazilian scene, such as Gabriel "FalleN" Toledo,Fernando "fer" Alvarenga, , Lincoln "fnx" Lau,Ricardo "boltz" Prass and Vinicius "VINI" Figueiredo. However, the team has changed over time. The organization also has a CrossFire team.

History

On September 5, 2018, Imperial acquired the Counter-Strike: Global Offensive team from Santos.
